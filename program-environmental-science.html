Bachelor of Environmental Science

Program Overview

The Environmental Science program explores the interaction between humans and the natural world. Students gain a deep understanding of ecological systems, sustainability practices, and the science behind environmental challenges and solutions.

What You'll Learn

  • Ecology and Biodiversity
  • Environmental Policy and Ethics
  • Climate Change and Sustainability
  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS)
  • Water, Soil, and Air Quality Management

Career Opportunities

Graduates pursue careers as:

  • Environmental Consultants
  • Wildlife or Conservation Scientists
  • Environmental Policy Analysts
  • Sustainability Coordinators
  • Ecological Field Researchers

Fieldwork & Research

Students engage in outdoor fieldwork, lab-based research, and environmental impact assessments, preparing for real-world environmental problem-solving.

Program Details

  • Degree: B.Sc. in Environmental Science
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Mode: On-Campus / Hybrid
  • Credits: 120
  • Language: English
  • Start Dates: September / January
