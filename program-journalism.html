Bachelor of Arts in Journalism & Media

Program Overview

The Journalism & Media program equips students with critical storytelling, investigative, and multimedia production skills. Learn to navigate the evolving media landscape with confidence, integrity, and technical expertise in both traditional and digital platforms.

What You'll Learn

  • News Writing and Investigative Reporting
  • Digital Media Production and Editing
  • Media Ethics and Law
  • Broadcast Journalism and Podcasting
  • Social Media Strategy and Analytics

Career Opportunities

Graduates pursue careers as:

  • Journalists and News Reporters
  • Media Producers and Editors
  • Social Media Managers
  • Broadcast Anchors or Radio Hosts
  • Content Creators and Media Consultants

Internships & Media Labs

Students gain practical experience through internships at news organizations, campus media outlets, and hands-on projects in digital media labs and production studios.

Program Details

  • Degree: B.A. in Journalism & Media
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Mode: On-Campus / Hybrid
  • Credits: 120
  • Language: English
  • Start Dates: September / January
