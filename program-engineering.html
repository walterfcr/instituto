Bachelor of Science in Engineering

Program Overview

The Engineering program offers a rigorous education in mathematics, science, and technical engineering principles. Students will gain problem-solving, innovation, and design skills essential to addressing complex engineering challenges in a variety of industries.

What You'll Learn

  • Engineering Mathematics and Physics
  • Thermodynamics and Fluid Mechanics
  • Structural Design and Materials Science
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Simulation
  • Project Management and Engineering Ethics

Career Opportunities

Graduates pursue careers as:

  • Civil Engineers
  • Mechanical Engineers
  • Electrical or Electronics Engineers
  • Project Engineers
  • R&D Specialists or Consultants

Internships & Capstone Projects

Students work on real-world engineering problems through internships, lab research, and final-year capstone design projects in collaboration with industry partners.

Program Details

  • Degree: B.Sc. in Engineering
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Mode: On-Campus / Hybrid
  • Credits: 120
  • Language: English
  • Start Dates: September / January
