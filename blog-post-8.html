Student Art Exhibit Opening

Published on August 2, 2025

Step into a world of creativity and imagination at the opening of our Student Art Exhibit. Featuring original works from our art and design students, this showcase celebrates expression, talent, and innovation across various mediums.

Attendees will enjoy a range of pieces including painting, sculpture, digital art, and mixed media. The exhibit offers a glimpse into the unique perspectives and evolving styles of our student artists.

Exhibit Highlights

  • Opening night reception with refreshments
  • Meet-and-greet with featured student artists
  • Guided tours of selected works
  • Live art performance and interactive installations
  • Gallery open to the public for two weeks

We invite art lovers, families, and the entire school community to support and celebrate the talents of our creative students.
