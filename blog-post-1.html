Open Day for New Students

Published on May 28, 2025

Join our campus tour and get to know our faculty and programs. This event is perfect for prospective students and their families to explore our facilities, meet instructors, and get answers to all your questions.

We will have guided tours, sample lectures, and fun activities throughout the day to help you experience what life is like at our campus.

Schedule

  • 10:00 AM – Welcome & Introduction
  • 11:00 AM – Campus Tour
  • 12:30 PM – Lunch & Networking
  • 2:00 PM – Sample Lectures
  • 4:00 PM – Q&A Session

Don’t miss this opportunity to start your educational journey with us!
