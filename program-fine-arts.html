Bachelor of Fine Arts

Program Overview

The Fine Arts program nurtures creative talent and artistic expression through hands-on studio work, art history, and contemporary theory. Students explore various media while developing their unique artistic voice and portfolio.

What You'll Learn

  • Drawing, Painting, and Sculpture
  • Art History and Visual Culture
  • Digital Media and Photography
  • Conceptual Art and Installation
  • Portfolio Development and Critique

Career Opportunities

Graduates pursue careers as:

  • Professional Artists
  • Gallery or Museum Curators
  • Illustrators and Designers
  • Art Teachers or Educators
  • Creative Directors or Art Therapists

Studios & Exhibitions

Students benefit from dedicated studio spaces, mentorship by practicing artists, and opportunities to exhibit their work in university galleries and public venues.

Program Details

  • Degree: B.F.A. in Fine Arts
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Mode: On-Campus / Hybrid
  • Credits: 120
  • Language: English
  • Start Dates: September / January
