Community Volunteer Day

Published on March 16, 2025

Make a meaningful impact by joining our annual Community Volunteer Day. This event brings together students, faculty, and staff for a full day dedicated to giving back to the neighborhoods that support us.

Volunteers will participate in a variety of activities, including park cleanups, food bank assistance, tutoring sessions, and senior outreach programs.

Ways You Can Help

  • Beautify local parks and public spaces
  • Sort and distribute goods at the community food pantry
  • Mentor younger students in after-school programs
  • Visit and assist residents at senior centers

All participants will receive a certificate of service and volunteer hours will be recorded. Join us in making a difference!
