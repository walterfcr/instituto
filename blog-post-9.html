Study Abroad Info Session

Published on March 20, 2025

Thinking of expanding your academic horizons internationally? Our Study Abroad Info Session is your gateway to learning about exciting exchange programs available in 2025.

This session will provide key insights into the application process, partner universities, eligibility criteria, and the enriching experiences that come with studying abroad.

Session Highlights

  • Overview of partner institutions across Europe, Asia, and the Americas
  • Step-by-step application guidance
  • Q&A panel with past exchange students
  • Scholarship and financial aid information
  • Networking opportunity with international program coordinators

Don’t miss your chance to discover how a semester abroad can shape your personal and academic journey. Open to all students interested in applying.
