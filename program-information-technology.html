Bachelor of Information Technology

Program Overview

The Information Technology program equips students with practical skills in computing, networking, and systems development. You'll learn to design, implement, and manage IT solutions to meet real-world business and technological challenges.

What You'll Learn

  • Computer Networks and Cybersecurity
  • Database Systems and Web Development
  • Cloud Computing and Virtualization
  • Programming Languages and Software Development
  • IT Project Management and Systems Analysis

Career Opportunities

Graduates pursue careers as:

  • IT Specialists or Systems Administrators
  • Software Developers or Web Programmers
  • Network or Security Analysts
  • Database Administrators
  • Tech Support Engineers or Consultants

Hands-On Learning

Students work on practical projects, labs, and internships in IT departments, tech firms, and start-ups, gaining industry-relevant experience and certifications.

Program Details

  • Degree: B.Sc. in Information Technology
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Mode: On-Campus / Hybrid
  • Credits: 120
  • Language: English
  • Start Dates: September / January
