Annual Science Fair 2025

Published on April 8, 2025

Experience the excitement of discovery and creativity at our Annual Science Fair 2025! This year's event brings together young innovators from across the school to showcase their projects, experiments, and groundbreaking ideas.

From robotics and environmental solutions to chemistry and physics, students will present months of hard work and research to peers, teachers, and special guests.

Event Features

  • Interactive project demonstrations
  • Guest judging panel with scientists and educators
  • Awards for innovation and scientific inquiry
  • Family-friendly science games and exhibits
  • Networking opportunities with STEM professionals

We encourage everyone to attend and support the next generation of scientists, engineers, and thinkers shaping our future.
