Graduation Ceremony 2025

Published on June 12, 2025

Celebrate the success of our graduating class in a memorable ceremony filled with pride, joy, and inspiration. Join us as we honor the achievements of our students and look ahead to their bright futures.

The event will include speeches from distinguished faculty and alumni, award presentations, and a heartfelt farewell to the class of 2025.

Event Highlights

  • Opening remarks by the Dean
  • Keynote speech by an honored alumnus
  • Diploma awarding ceremony
  • Musical performances by the student choir
  • Reception with refreshments

We welcome families, friends, and the entire school community to come together and make this event truly special.
