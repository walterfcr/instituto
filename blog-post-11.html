Sports Week Kickoff

Published on September 25, 2025

Get ready for a high-energy week filled with sports, spirit, and camaraderie! Our annual Sports Week is back, and it's bigger than ever.

Students, faculty, and staff are invited to participate in a variety of tournaments, including basketball, volleyball, track and field, and more.

Event Highlights

  • Opening Parade & Torch Lighting Ceremony
  • Interdepartmental Sports Tournaments
  • Fitness Workshops and Wellness Seminars
  • Finals and Awards Ceremony on Sunday

Whether you're competing, cheering from the sidelines, or volunteering behind the scenes, there's a role for everyone. Let’s make this week unforgettable!
