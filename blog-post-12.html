New Science Labs Inauguration

Published on February 19, 2025

We’re excited to announce the grand opening of our newly upgraded science laboratories! Designed with cutting-edge technology and modern equipment, these labs will revolutionize hands-on learning and research opportunities on campus.

The inauguration ceremony welcomed students, faculty, alumni, and guests, with guided tours and live demonstrations showcasing the capabilities of the new facilities.

Lab Features

  • Advanced chemistry and physics labs
  • Dedicated research and innovation zones
  • Eco-friendly infrastructure with energy-efficient design
  • Enhanced safety systems and digital integration

These state-of-the-art labs reflect our commitment to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and discovery in STEM education.
