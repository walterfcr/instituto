Sustainability Week Recap

Published on April 25, 2025

Sustainability Week was a resounding success, bringing our campus community together to promote eco-conscious living and environmental responsibility. Through a series of interactive workshops, talks, and green initiatives, students and staff engaged in meaningful actions for a better planet.

From planting trees and reducing plastic use to learning about renewable energy and sustainable diets, the week offered a variety of opportunities to contribute to global and local sustainability efforts.

Event Highlights

  • Campus Clean-Up Drive
  • Zero-Waste Challenge and Eco-Fair
  • Guest Lecture on Climate Change Solutions
  • DIY Upcycling Workshops
  • Bike-to-Campus Campaign

Thank you to everyone who participated. Let’s continue making small changes that lead to a big impact.
